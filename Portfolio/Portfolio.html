College Information Chatbot

The college information chatbot is an advanced AI system designed to provide prospective students with comprehensive and personalized information about colleges and universities, streamlining the college search process. By leveraging fine-tuned generative algorithms and extensive analytics, the chatbot continuously improves its functionalities and tailors responses to individual needs, creating a valuable resource for students and optimizing the admissions staff's efficiency.

Extremist Post Detection Using Deep Learning

Extremist Groups are using social media sites like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to increasingly pro- mote their ideologies and recruit individuals for terrorist activities. This work aims at reducing the impact of such posts by classifying them into two: extremist and non-extremist classes. Using a LSTM + RNN model we train the classification model to classify the posts as extremist and non extremist.
